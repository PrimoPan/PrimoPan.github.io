Dongyijie Pan (PrimoPan)

I am a senior student currently studying at the Communication University of China. I was a key competitor in the school's ACM/ICPC team and have secured top results for the university in major competitions such as ICPC/CCPC (Silver medal at 7th place, ranked 23rd in the university, which is the best result ever achieved by the Communication University of China in such competitions). My Codeforces Rating stands at 2151. With a solid foundation in coding, mathematics, and physics, I believe I will excel in my future research endeavors.
Additionally, my major is ranked first in China for digital media and human-computer interaction studies. Here, I have also learned a plethora of courses related to sketching, design, product modeling, and even psychology. These courses may seem disparate at first glance, but they have greatly enhanced both my technical and design thinking. I aspire to become an outstanding human-computer interaction researcher.
My current research interests include human-computer interaction and ubiquitous computing, especially using multimodal and augmented reality technologies to encourage users to change their daily behavioral habits (both physically and mentally). This can potentially alleviate the conditions of individuals with mild mental disorders to a certain extent.
I am currently working on the development of an improvisational music teaching therapy for children with autism in Professor Fan Min's IDC Lab. If you are interested, please feel free to email me for further discussion.
You can email me at primojaypan@cuc.edu.cn
